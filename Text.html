Step 05 — Smart Notes

Quick Revision Bullets

Complex Numbers
  • z = a+bi · a=Real, b=Imaginary
  • Equality: both Real AND Imaginary must match
  • |z| is always non-negative real
  • Arg θ: use arctan(b/a) + adjust quadrant
  • Divide: multiply top & bottom by conjugate
  • i powers: divide exponent by 4, use remainder
  • EXAM TIP: state r AND θ clearly in polar form
Matrices
  • Expand det 3×3 along row with most zeros
  • Cramer: D=det(A); Dₓ=replace col1 with B
  • A⁻¹ = (1/detA) × adj(A)
  • adj(A) = transpose of cofactor matrix
  • Consistent: D≠0 (unique) or D=Dₓ=0 (∞)
  • EXAM TIP: always check det(A) first
Sequences & Series
  • AP: constant difference d between terms
  • GP: constant ratio r between terms
  • HP: reciprocals of terms form an AP
  • AM ≥ GM ≥ HM (positive unequal numbers)
  • Infinite GP: MUST have |r| < 1
  • 0.363636… = 36/99 = 4/11 (GP trick)
  • Σn, Σn², Σn³ — MEMORIZE all three
Induction & Binomial
  • 3 steps: Base (n=1) → Assume (n=k) → Prove (n=k+1)
  • NEVER just verify — algebraically show P(k)⟹P(k+1)
  • General term: T(r+1) = ⁿCᵣ · aⁿ⁻ʳ · bʳ
  • Indep. of x: set total power of x = 0, solve r
  • n even → 1 middle term; n odd → 2 middle terms
  • Divisibility: show f(k+1) − f(k) is divisible
Trigonometry
  • Memorize cos(A−B) first — all others derive from it
  • Allied: odd multiple of 90° → function changes
  • sin↔cos, tan↔cot when multiplied by odd 90°
  • Double angle: sin2A and cos2A most asked
  • Exact values: use 30°, 45°, 60° + compound
  • Product-to-sum: for proving identities
P&C + Polynomials
  • ⁿPᵣ = n!/(n−r)! — order MATTERS
  • ⁿCᵣ = n!/[r!(n−r)!] — order does NOT matter
  • Circular: (n−1)! arrangements
  • Remainder Theorem: p(a) = remainder when ÷(x−a)
  • Factor Theorem: p(a)=0 ⟺ (x−a) is a factor

❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid
